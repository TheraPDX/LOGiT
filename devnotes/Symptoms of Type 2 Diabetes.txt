Symptoms of Type 2 Diabetes
* according to Mayo Clinic
src: http://www.mayoclinic.org/diseases-conditions/prediabetes/basics/symptoms/con-20024420

Symptoms that indicate you've moved from pre-diabetes to type 2 diabetes:
*See your doctor if you're experiencing any of the following symptoms on a regular basis

1. increased thirst
2. frequent urination
3. fatigue
4. blurred vision
